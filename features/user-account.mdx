## Creating a TMDB Account

If you don't have a TMDB account yet, you'll need to create one:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Login">
    Click the **Login** button in the Popcorn Vision navigation bar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Don't have an account?'">
    On the login page, click the **"Doesn't have an account?"** link at the bottom
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete TMDB Signup">
    You'll be redirected to TMDB's signup page. Fill in:
    - Username (must be unique)
    - Password (minimum 4 characters)
    - Email address
    - Accept terms and conditions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your email for a verification link from TMDB and click it to activate your account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Popcorn Vision">
    Come back to Popcorn Vision and log in with your new credentials
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your TMDB account is completely free and gives you access to features across all TMDB-powered applications.
</Info>

## Logging In

Once you have a TMDB account, logging into Popcorn Vision is simple:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Login Page">
    Click the **Login** button in the navigation bar or visit `/login`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Enter your TMDB username and password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click the **Login** button to authenticate
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Redirect">
    You'll be automatically redirected to the page you were viewing (or home page)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If you see an error message like "Invalid username or password", double-check your credentials. Use the "Forgot your password?" link if needed.
</Warning>

## Authentication Flow

Behind the scenes, Popcorn Vision uses TMDB's secure authentication process:

1. **Request Token**: Popcorn Vision requests a temporary token from TMDB
2. **Validate Credentials**: Your username and password are validated with the token
3. **Create Session**: A session ID is created for your authenticated session
4. **Store Session**: The session is securely stored to keep you logged in

<Note>
  Your password is never stored by Popcorn Vision. All authentication is handled directly by TMDB's secure API.
</Note>

## Password Reset

Forgot your password? No problem:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot your password?'">
    On the login page, click the **"Forgot your password?"** link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset on TMDB">
    You'll be redirected to TMDB's password reset page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email or Username">
    Provide your email address or username
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    TMDB will send password reset instructions to your email
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Login">
    After resetting your password, return to Popcorn Vision and log in
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your Profile

Once logged in, access your profile at `/profile`:

### Profile Information

Your profile displays:

- **Display Name**: Your name (if set) or username
- **Username**: Your TMDB username
- **Member Since**: Account creation date
- **Avatar**: Your TMDB profile picture (if set)

<Tip>
  To update your profile information or avatar, visit your TMDB account settings. Changes will automatically sync to Popcorn Vision.
</Tip>

### Activity Overview

Your profile shows three main collections:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Favorites">
    All movies and TV shows you've marked as favorites
    
    - Quick access to your favorite content
    - Sorted by when you added them (newest first by default)
    - Filter by movies or TV shows
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Watchlist">
    Content you want to watch later
    
    - Movies and shows you've bookmarked
    - Organized by date added
    - Easy management with one-click removal
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Rated">
    Everything you've rated
    
    - All your movie and TV show ratings
    - Shows your personal rating alongside community rating
    - Sort by rating, release date, or popularity
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Sorting Your Collections

Each collection can be sorted using the dropdown in the top-right:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Created At" icon="clock">
    Date added to your collection (default: newest first)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rating" icon="star">
    Sort by your rating or average rating
  </Card>
  <Card title="Popularity" icon="fire">
    Most to least popular
  </Card>
  <Card title="Release Date" icon="calendar">
    Newest to oldest releases
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Sort order options:**
- **Descending**: High to low, newest to oldest (default)
- **Ascending**: Low to high, oldest to newest

### Filtering Between Movies and TV

Switch between content types using the dropdown:

- **Movies**: Shows only movies in your collections
- **TV Shows**: Shows only TV shows in your collections

<Info>
  The filter applies to all three collections (Favorites, Watchlist, Rated) simultaneously.
</Info>

## Account Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What data does Popcorn Vision access?">
    Popcorn Vision only accesses:
    - Your TMDB username and profile info
    - Your watchlist items
    - Your favorite items
    - Your ratings
    
    No personal information like email or password is accessed or stored.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is my data shared with third parties?">
    No. Your TMDB account data is only used to provide features within Popcorn Vision. No data is sold or shared with third parties.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I delete my data?">
    Yes. To remove your data:
    1. Remove items from your watchlist and favorites in Popcorn Vision
    2. Delete your ratings
    3. Log out of Popcorn Vision
    
    To permanently delete your account, you'll need to delete your TMDB account at TMDB.org.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when I log out?">
    Logging out removes your session from Popcorn Vision. Your data remains safe in your TMDB account and will be available when you log back in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Logging Out

To log out of Popcorn Vision:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Your Profile Icon">
    Click on your profile picture or username in the navigation bar
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Logout">
    Click the **Logout** button in the dropdown menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    You'll be logged out and redirected to the home page
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Logging out only ends your Popcorn Vision session. Your TMDB account remains active for use on TMDB.org and other TMDB-powered apps.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Login button doesn't work">
    Try these steps:
    1. Make sure you've entered both username and password
    2. Check that your credentials are correct
    3. Verify your TMDB account is activated (check email)
    4. Clear your browser cache and try again
    5. Try a different browser
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stuck on loading after login">
    This usually indicates a network or session issue:
    1. Wait 30 seconds for the process to complete
    2. Refresh the page
    3. Clear cookies and try logging in again
    4. Check your internet connection
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Changes not syncing to TMDB">
    If your actions in Popcorn Vision aren't appearing on TMDB:
    1. Log out and log back in to refresh your session
    2. Check that you're logged into the correct account
    3. Give it a few minutes - sync can take time
    4. Verify the action completed successfully (no error messages)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Invalid username or password error">
    Common solutions:
    1. Double-check your username (not email)
    2. Verify password is correct (case-sensitive)
    3. Make sure your account is activated
    4. Use the "Forgot password" link if needed
    5. Check if your TMDB account is still active
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
