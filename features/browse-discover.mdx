Popcorn Vision makes it easy to discover your next favorite movie or TV show through curated content sections and powerful browsing capabilities.

## Home Page Hero Slider

When you first open Popcorn Vision, you'll see a full-screen hero slider featuring the top trending titles.

<Info>
  The hero slider showcases 5 trending movies or TV shows with stunning backdrop images and quick access to details.
</Info>

**What you'll see:**
- Full-screen backdrop images for an immersive experience
- Title logo overlays when available
- Key information: genre, release date, runtime, and rating
- Quick action buttons: Add to Watchlist, Mark as Favorite, and Rate
- Thumbnail navigation on desktop for quick browsing

<Tip>
  Use your keyboard arrow keys or swipe on mobile to navigate through the hero slider!
</Tip>

## Browse by Category

Popcorn Vision organizes content into several curated categories on the home page:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Now Playing" icon="play">
    Movies currently in theaters or TV shows currently airing (last 3 months)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Upcoming" icon="calendar">
    Movies and shows releasing in the next 3 months
  </Card>
  <Card title="Top Rated" icon="star">
    Highest-rated content by vote count and average rating
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trending" icon="fire">
    What's hot this week across movies and TV shows
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Navigating Categories

Each category displays content in a horizontal scrolling slider:

1. **Scroll horizontally** to browse through titles
2. **Click on any poster** to view full details
3. **Use "View All"** button to see the complete list with applied filters

<Note>
  Clicking "View All" on any category takes you to the advanced search page with relevant filters pre-applied.
</Note>

## Browse by Production Company

For movies, browse content from major production studios:

- **Warner Bros. Pictures** - Major Hollywood blockbusters
- **DreamWorks Animation** - Animated family favorites
- **Blumhouse Productions** - Horror and thriller films

Each company has a dedicated section on the home page showcasing their latest and most popular releases.

## Browse by TV Network

For TV shows, explore content from popular networks and streaming platforms:

- Major broadcast networks (ABC, CBS, NBC, FOX)
- Premium cable channels (HBO, Showtime, Starz)
- Streaming platforms (Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hulu, Disney+)

<Tip>
  Use the Network filter in the [advanced search](/features/search-filters) to find shows from specific networks.
</Tip>

## Trending Spotlight

Between category sections, you'll find **Trending Spotlight** cards that highlight individual trending titles with:

- Large poster and backdrop images
- Complete overview and synopsis
- All key details and metadata
- Quick action buttons

<Note>
  The home page layout alternates between horizontal sliders and trending spotlights for an engaging browsing experience.
</Note>

## Switching Between Movies and TV

Popcorn Vision has separate home pages for movies and TV shows:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Movies">
    Access the movies home page at `/` (root URL)
    
    Features:
    - Now Playing (in theaters)
    - Upcoming releases
    - Browse by production company
    - Movie-specific genres
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="TV Shows">
    Access the TV shows home page at `/tv`
    
    Features:
    - On The Air (currently airing)
    - Upcoming episodes
    - Browse by network
    - TV-specific genres
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Tips for Effective Browsing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Finding Similar Content">
    When viewing a movie or show's detail page, check the "You May Also Like" and "Recommendations" sections for similar titles based on genre, cast, and themes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Exploring Collections">
    Many movies belong to collections (like Marvel Cinematic Universe or Harry Potter). When viewing a movie that's part of a collection, you'll see the entire collection displayed on the detail page.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Using View All Links">
    Every content slider includes a "View All" link that takes you to the search page with relevant filters pre-applied, making it easy to dive deeper into that category.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Keyboard Navigation">
    On desktop, use arrow keys to navigate the hero slider and spacebar to pause/resume autoplay (when enabled).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
