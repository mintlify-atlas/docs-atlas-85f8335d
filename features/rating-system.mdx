Popcorn Vision's rating system lets you rate movies and TV shows on a 10-point scale. Your ratings sync with TMDB and contribute to the community database.

<Info>
  You need a TMDB account to rate content. [Learn how to create an account](/features/user-account).
</Info>

## Understanding the Rating Scale

Popcorn Vision uses a **10-point rating scale** (0.5 to 10 stars):

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="1-3 Stars" icon="face-frown">
    **Poor to Below Average**
    
    Not recommended, significant issues
  </Card>
  <Card title="4-6 Stars" icon="face-meh">
    **Average to Good**
    
    Decent but flawed, somewhat enjoyable
  </Card>
  <Card title="7-8 Stars" icon="face-smile">
    **Great**
    
    Highly enjoyable, well-made, recommended
  </Card>
  <Card title="9-10 Stars" icon="face-laugh-beam">
    **Exceptional**
    
    Outstanding, must-watch, near-perfect
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Half-Star Ratings

The rating system uses **half-star increments**:

- ⭐ 0.5 = half star
- ⭐ 1.0 = one star
- ⭐ 1.5 = one and a half stars
- ... up to 10 stars

This gives you 20 different rating options (0.5, 1.0, 1.5, 2.0... up to 10.0) for precise ratings.

## How to Rate Content

### Rating Movies and TV Shows

Rate any movie or TV show from its detail page:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Content">
    Go to the detail page of any movie or TV show you want to rate
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Rating Section">
    Scroll down to find the **"Your Rating"** section (usually near user actions)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click a Star">
    Click on any of the 10 stars to set your rating:
    - Click the **left half** of a star for a half-star rating (e.g., 7.5)
    - Click the **right half** for a full-star rating (e.g., 8.0)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    The rating is saved instantly - a loading spinner confirms the action
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Hover over the stars to preview your rating before clicking. The stars highlight to show what rating you'll give.
</Tip>

### Rating Interaction

The rating interface provides visual feedback:

- **Hover**: Stars highlight in yellow to preview your rating
- **Click**: Stars fill in yellow to show your selected rating
- **Loading**: A spinner appears while saving your rating
- **Success**: The filled stars remain to show your rating is saved

### Rating from Quick Actions

You can also rate content from:

- **Home slider**: Rate featured content directly from the hero slider
- **Trending cards**: Rate trending movies and shows inline
- **Search results**: Some views include quick rating options

<Note>
  Not logged in? Clicking to rate will prompt you to log in first.
</Note>

## Viewing Your Ratings

### On Content Pages

When viewing a movie or show you've rated:

- Your rating appears as filled yellow stars
- The number of stars shows your rating
- The rating persists between sessions

### On Your Profile

See all your ratings on your profile:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    Navigate to `/profile` via your profile menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Rated Section">
    Your rated content appears in the **"Rated"** section
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Details">
    Each item shows:
    - Movie/show poster and title
    - Your personal rating (e.g., "⭐ 8.5")
    - Community average rating
    - Release year
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Sorting Rated Content

Organize your rated content by:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Your Rating">
    **Sort by your personal ratings:**
    - Descending: Your highest-rated first
    - Ascending: Your lowest-rated first
    
    Great for finding your favorite movies or seeing what you didn't like.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Created At">
    **Sort by when you rated it:**
    - Descending: Most recently rated first
    - Ascending: Oldest ratings first
    
    Perfect for tracking your rating history.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Release Date">
    **Sort by original release:**
    - Descending: Newest releases first
    - Ascending: Oldest releases first
    
    Useful for seeing ratings across different eras.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Popularity">
    **Sort by current popularity:**
    - Descending: Most popular first
    - Ascending: Least popular first
    
    Find which of your ratings are for trending content.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Changing Your Rating

Update your rating anytime:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Return to Content">
    Go back to the movie or show's detail page
  </Step>
  <Step title="See Current Rating">
    Your current rating displays as filled yellow stars
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click a Different Star">
    Click a different star value to change your rating
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auto-Save">
    The new rating saves immediately, replacing your old rating
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  You can change your rating as many times as you want. Only your most recent rating counts.
</Info>

## Removing Your Rating

Delete a rating if you want to remove it:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From Detail Page">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find Your Rating">
        Go to the content's detail page where your rating is displayed
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Delete Icon">
        Look for the **trash can icon** (🗑️) next to your rating
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
        Click the icon - your rating is removed immediately
      </Step>
      <Step title="Stars Reset">
        The stars become unfilled, indicating no rating
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From Profile">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to Profile">
        Navigate to `/profile`
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find in Rated Section">
        Locate the item in your **Rated** section
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Delete">
        Click the trash icon on that item
      </Step>
      <Step title="Removed">
        The item is removed from your rated list
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  Deleting a rating is permanent. You'll need to rate the content again if you change your mind.
</Warning>

## How Ratings Work

### Your Rating vs. Community Rating

Popcorn Vision displays two types of ratings:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Your Personal Rating" icon="user">
    - Yellow stars (⭐)
    - Shows your opinion
    - Only visible to you
    - Syncs with TMDB
    - Appears in your "Rated" section
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community Average" icon="users">
    - Usually displayed as a number (e.g., 7.8/10)
    - Average of all TMDB user ratings
    - Publicly visible
    - Updates as more people rate
    - Includes your rating in the average
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Contributing to TMDB

When you rate content in Popcorn Vision:

1. **Your rating is saved** to your TMDB account
2. **It contributes** to the community average rating on TMDB
3. **It helps others** discover quality content
4. **It's synced** across TMDB-powered applications

<Info>
  Your individual rating is private, but it contributes anonymously to the public average rating that everyone sees.
</Info>

## Rating TV Show Seasons and Episodes

Beyond rating the entire TV show, you can rate individual seasons and episodes:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Rating a Show">
    Rate the overall TV series from its main detail page:
    
    - Reflects your opinion of the show as a whole
    - Appears in your profile's rated section
    - Contributes to the show's overall rating
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Rating Seasons">
    Rate individual seasons:
    
    - Navigate to a specific season page
    - Rate that season independently
    - Useful when show quality varies by season
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Rating Episodes">
    Rate individual episodes:
    
    - View a specific episode's details
    - Rate that episode alone
    - Perfect for standout or disappointing episodes
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  You can rate the show, all seasons, AND all episodes separately - they're independent ratings.
</Tip>

## Syncing with TMDB

Your ratings automatically sync with TMDB:

### From Popcorn Vision to TMDB

- Ratings made in Popcorn Vision appear on TMDB.org within seconds
- They contribute to community averages immediately
- They appear in your TMDB rating history

### From TMDB to Popcorn Vision

- Ratings made on TMDB.org sync to Popcorn Vision
- Refresh the page to see ratings from TMDB
- Log out and back in if sync seems delayed

<Note>
  Real-time sync applies to ratings you make in Popcorn Vision. For ratings made elsewhere, refresh your session to see updates.
</Note>

## Best Practices for Rating

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Rate After Watching">
    Rate content only after you've watched it completely:
    
    ✅ Finish the movie or show first  
    ✅ Let your opinion settle (wait a day if needed)  
    ✅ Consider the entire experience  
    
    ❌ Don't rate based on trailers  
    ❌ Don't rate if you haven't finished it  
    ❌ Don't rate based on others' opinions  
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use the Full Scale">
    Don't cluster all ratings around 7-8:
    
    - Use 1-3 for content you disliked
    - Use 4-6 for average content
    - Use 7-8 for content you enjoyed
    - Save 9-10 for truly exceptional content
    
    A diverse rating scale is more useful for you and others.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Be Honest">
    Rate based on your genuine opinion:
    
    - Don't inflate ratings for fan favorites
    - Don't tank ratings for popular content you dislike
    - Your honest rating helps the community
    - It also helps you remember what you truly thought
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Update Old Ratings">
    Your opinion may change over time:
    
    - Revisit old ratings occasionally
    - Update if your opinion has changed
    - Consider rewatching before changing
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Combine with Notes">
    Consider keeping separate notes about WHY you gave certain ratings:
    
    - The rating number is just a summary
    - Notes help you remember details
    - Useful for recommendations to friends
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Understanding Rating Statistics

When viewing content, you'll see various rating statistics:

### Average Rating

- **Scale**: 0-10
- **Source**: Average of all TMDB user ratings
- **Display**: Often shown as a number (e.g., 7.8) with star icon

### Vote Count

- **What it is**: Number of users who rated the content
- **Why it matters**: High vote counts = more reliable average
- **Display**: Usually shown as "1.2K votes" or similar

<Tip>
  A movie with 8.5 rating from 10,000 votes is more reliably "great" than one with 8.5 from 100 votes.
</Tip>

## Privacy & Visibility

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can see my ratings?">
    - Your individual ratings are visible on your TMDB profile (based on TMDB privacy settings)
    - They contribute anonymously to community averages
    - In Popcorn Vision, only you see your personal ratings in your profile
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I make my ratings private?">
    Rating privacy is controlled by your TMDB account settings:
    
    1. Go to TMDB.org
    2. Visit your account settings
    3. Adjust privacy preferences
    4. Changes apply across TMDB-powered apps
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are ratings anonymous?">
    Your ratings contribute to community averages anonymously, but your TMDB profile shows your rating history according to your TMDB privacy settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Rating doesn't save">
    If your rating isn't saved:
    
    1. Make sure you're logged in
    2. Check your internet connection
    3. Wait for the loading spinner to complete
    4. Refresh and try again
    5. Try rating from the detail page instead of quick actions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't see my rating">
    If your rating doesn't appear:
    
    1. Refresh the page
    2. Log out and log back in
    3. Verify you're using the correct account
    4. Check if the rating shows on TMDB.org
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Delete button doesn't work">
    If you can't delete a rating:
    
    1. Look for the trash icon next to the stars
    2. Make sure you clicked the icon, not the stars
    3. Wait for loading to complete
    4. Refresh and try again from the detail page
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Rating not syncing to TMDB">
    If your Popcorn Vision rating doesn't appear on TMDB:
    
    1. Give it a few minutes
    2. Refresh TMDB.org
    3. Clear browser cache
    4. Log out and back in on both platforms
    5. Verify the rating saved in Popcorn Vision (check your profile)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
