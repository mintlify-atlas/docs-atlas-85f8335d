Keep track of content you want to watch later and organize your favorite movies and TV shows with Popcorn Vision's watchlist and favorites features.

<Info>
  You need a TMDB account to use watchlist and favorites. [Learn how to create an account](/features/user-account).
</Info>

## Watchlist

Your watchlist is a personal collection of movies and TV shows you want to watch later.

### Adding to Watchlist

There are several ways to add content to your watchlist:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From Detail Page">
    When viewing a movie or TV show:
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find the Watchlist Button">
        Look for the **bookmark icon** button near the top of the page
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click to Add">
        Click the button - it will change from an outlined bookmark to a filled bookmark
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm">
        The button text changes to indicate it's in your watchlist
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From Home Slider">
    On the home page hero slider:
    
    1. Navigate to a movie/show you're interested in
    2. Look for action buttons below the title
    3. Click the **Watchlist** button with bookmark icon
    4. Button updates to show it's added
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From Trending Cards">
    In trending spotlight sections:
    
    1. Find the watchlist button among the action buttons
    2. Click to add instantly
    3. Visual feedback confirms addition
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  When you're not logged in, clicking the watchlist button will prompt you to log in first.
</Tip>

### Removing from Watchlist

To remove an item from your watchlist:

1. Click the **filled bookmark icon** on any page where you see it
2. The icon changes back to an outlined bookmark
3. The item is instantly removed from your watchlist

**Or from your profile:**

1. Go to `/profile`
2. Scroll to your **Watchlist** section
3. Find the item you want to remove
4. Click the **bookmark icon** on that item
5. It's immediately removed from the list

### Viewing Your Watchlist

Access your complete watchlist:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile">
    Click your profile icon in the navigation bar and select **Profile**, or go directly to `/profile`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Watchlist Section">
    Your watchlist appears as one of three sections on your profile
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Content">
    Scroll through your saved items with:
    - Poster images
    - Title and release year
    - Rating information
    - Quick access to full details
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Organizing Your Watchlist

Sort your watchlist to find what you want to watch:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Created At" icon="clock">
    When you added it (newest first by default)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Release Date" icon="calendar">
    When it was released or will air
  </Card>
  <Card title="Popularity" icon="fire">
    How popular it is right now
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rating" icon="star">
    Community rating (highest first)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**To change sorting:**

1. Use the **Sort** dropdown in the top-right of your profile
2. Select your preferred sort method
3. Toggle between **Ascending** ↑ and **Descending** ↓ order
4. Your choice applies to all collections

<Note>
  Sorting by "Created At" in descending order shows what you most recently added - perfect for finding that movie you just bookmarked!
</Note>

## Favorites

Favorites are movies and TV shows you love and want to mark as your personal favorites.

### Adding to Favorites

Mark content as a favorite from any page:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From Detail Page">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find the Favorite Button">
        Look for the **star icon** button (usually near the watchlist button)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click to Favorite">
        Click the button - the star fills in with color
      </Step>
      <Step title="Instant Save">
        The item is immediately added to your favorites
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From Hero Slider">
    1. Navigate to a movie/show on the home slider
    2. Click the **Favorite** button with star icon
    3. Star icon fills to confirm
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="From Trending">
    1. Find the favorite button on trending cards
    2. Click to add to favorites
    3. Button updates immediately
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  The favorite button shows a filled yellow star when an item is in your favorites.
</Info>

### Removing from Favorites

Unfavorite content just as easily:

1. Click the **filled star icon** anywhere you see it
2. The star becomes outlined again
3. The item is removed from your favorites

**Or from your profile:**

1. Visit `/profile`
2. Find the item in your **Favorites** section
3. Click the **star icon** on that item
4. It's immediately removed

### Viewing Your Favorites

See all your favorite movies and shows:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Profile">
    Navigate to `/profile` via your profile menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Favorites Section">
    Your favorites appear in the first section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Collection">
    View all favorited content with poster grids and quick access to details
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Organizing Favorites

Sort your favorites using the same options as watchlist:

- **Created At**: When you favorited it
- **Release Date**: Original release date
- **Popularity**: Current popularity
- **Rating**: Community rating

Change sort order using the dropdown menu on your profile.

## Filtering by Content Type

Both watchlist and favorites support filtering:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Type Filter">
    On your profile page, find the content type dropdown (usually in the top bar)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Type">
    Choose:
    - **Movies**: Show only movies
    - **TV Shows**: Show only TV shows
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Filtered Results">
    All three sections (Favorites, Watchlist, Rated) update to show only the selected type
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use this filter when you're specifically looking for a movie to watch tonight or a new series to start.
</Tip>

## Watchlist vs. Favorites: Which to Use?

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Use Watchlist For">
    ✅ Movies/shows you haven't watched yet  
    ✅ Content you want to watch later  
    ✅ Temporary bookmarks  
    ✅ Recommendations from friends  
    ✅ Upcoming releases you're interested in  
    
    **Think of it as**: "To watch queue"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Use Favorites For">
    ✅ Movies/shows you've watched and loved  
    ✅ Content you'd watch again  
    ✅ All-time favorites  
    ✅ Movies to recommend to others  
    ✅ Building your personal collection  
    
    **Think of it as**: "My favorites hall of fame"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Use Both For">
    Many users use both features together:
    
    1. Add content to **Watchlist** when you discover it
    2. Watch the movie or show
    3. If you loved it, remove from watchlist and add to **Favorites**
    4. If it was just okay, remove from watchlist
    
    This workflow keeps your lists organized and purposeful.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing Your Collections

### Bulk Viewing

Your profile displays up to 20 items per section initially:

- Scroll down within each section to load more
- Infinite scroll loads additional items automatically
- Loading indicators show when fetching more content

### Item Details

Each item in your collections shows:

- **Poster image**: Visual identification
- **Title**: Movie or show name
- **Year**: Release year
- **Rating**: Community average rating (⭐ score)
- **Your rating**: If you've rated it (shown on rated items)

<Info>
  Click any item to view its full detail page with complete information, cast, crew, trailers, and more.
</Info>

### Quick Actions

On your profile, each item has quick action buttons:

- **Bookmark icon**: Remove from watchlist
- **Star icon**: Remove from favorites
- **Trash icon**: Delete your rating (on rated items)

## Syncing with TMDB

Your watchlist and favorites automatically sync with TMDB:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="From Popcorn Vision to TMDB" icon="arrow-right">
    Actions in Popcorn Vision appear on TMDB.org within seconds
  </Card>
  <Card title="From TMDB to Popcorn Vision" icon="arrow-left">
    Changes on TMDB sync to Popcorn Vision when you refresh or log in
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  Sync happens in real-time for actions you take in Popcorn Vision. If you make changes on TMDB.org, refresh your Popcorn Vision profile to see updates.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep Your Watchlist Fresh">
    Regularly review your watchlist and remove items you:
    - Have already watched
    - Are no longer interested in
    - Should move to favorites
    
    This keeps your watchlist actionable and focused.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use Favorites as Your Personal IMDB">
    Build your favorites collection as a curated list of movies and shows you'd recommend. It becomes a great reference when:
    - Friends ask for recommendations
    - You want to rewatch something
    - You're showing off your taste in films
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Combine with Ratings">
    Add items to favorites AND rate them. This:
    - Helps you remember why it's a favorite
    - Contributes to TMDB's community ratings
    - Lets you sort favorites by your personal ratings
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Tag Upcoming Releases">
    Add upcoming movies and shows to your watchlist as soon as they're announced. Then:
    - Sort by release date to see what's coming soon
    - Get reminded when scrolling your watchlist
    - Be ready when they're released
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Visibility

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can see my watchlist and favorites?">
    Your Popcorn Vision watchlist and favorites are private to you. However, since they sync with TMDB, your TMDB privacy settings apply:
    
    - By default, TMDB lists are public
    - You can change privacy settings at TMDB.org
    - Check TMDB's privacy documentation for details
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I share my favorites?">
    While Popcorn Vision doesn't have built-in sharing, your favorites are synced to TMDB where they may be visible based on your TMDB privacy settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Button doesn't respond when clicked">
    If the watchlist or favorite button doesn't work:
    
    1. Make sure you're logged in
    2. Check your internet connection
    3. Wait a moment - it may be processing
    4. Refresh the page and try again
    5. Log out and log back in
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Items not appearing in profile">
    If content you added doesn't show in your profile:
    
    1. Refresh the profile page
    2. Check the content type filter (Movies vs. TV)
    3. Verify the item was successfully added (button filled)
    4. Log out and log in to refresh your session
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Changes not syncing to TMDB">
    If your Popcorn Vision lists don't match TMDB.org:
    
    1. Give it a few minutes for sync
    2. Refresh both pages
    3. Log out and log back in on both platforms
    4. Verify you're using the same TMDB account
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't remove items from lists">
    If clicking the filled icon doesn't remove the item:
    
    1. Make sure you're clicking the correct icon
    2. Wait for the loading spinner to complete
    3. Refresh the page to verify the change
    4. Try again from the detail page instead of profile
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
