## What is Popcorn Vision?

Popcorn Vision is a comprehensive movie and TV show streaming and discovery platform built with Next.js 14. It provides an extensive database powered by TMDB API, allowing users to explore, track, and manage their favorite content with ease.

Whether you're looking for the latest blockbuster, tracking your favorite TV series, or discovering hidden gems, Popcorn Vision offers a seamless experience for all your entertainment needs.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Authentication & User Features" icon="user">
    - **TMDB Authentication**: Secure login with TMDB API
    - **Favorites**: Save your favorite movies and TV shows
    - **Watchlist**: Create and manage a personalized watchlist
    - **Ratings**: Rate movies and TV shows to track your preferences
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Extensive Content Database" icon="film">
    - **Movies & TV Shows**: Access a vast collection powered by TMDB
    - **Detailed Information**: View synopsis, trailers, cast & crew details
    - **TV Show Tracking**: Monitor seasons, episodes, and show status
    - **Watch Trailers**: Preview content before watching
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Advanced Search & Filtering" icon="magnifying-glass">
    - **Multi-factor Search**: Filter by genre, release date, streaming platform
    - **Cast & Crew Search**: Find content by actor, director, or crew
    - **Technical Filters**: Runtime, rating, rating count, language
    - **TV-Specific Filters**: Network, status, type
    - **Company Search**: Discover content by production company
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Smart Recommendations" icon="stars">
    - **Trending Content**: See what's popular this week
    - **Now Playing**: Current releases in theaters
    - **Upcoming**: Stay updated on future releases
    - **Top Rated**: Discover critically acclaimed content
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Get Started

Ready to dive in? Follow our [Quickstart Guide](/quickstart) to get Popcorn Vision running locally in minutes, or check out the [Installation Guide](/installation) for detailed setup instructions.

<Note>
  **Need a TMDB API Key?** You'll need to create a free account at [The Movie Database](https://www.themoviedb.org/) to get your API credentials. Don't worry - we'll walk you through the entire process in the Quickstart guide.
</Note>
